What is the best type of roofing shingles?

Asphalt shingles are the most popular choice because of their affordable price and durability. However, we offer a variety of shingle types to suit your needs and preferences.

Types of Shingles

  • Strip Shingles:
    Basic, inexpensive and last between 15 and 20 years.
  • Dimensional Shingles:
    More aesthetically pleasing, lasting 20 to 30 years.
  • Luxury Shingles:
    Premium quality, with a duration of 25 to 40 years.

Understanding the differences between these types helps you make an informed decision for your household.

Cost and Durability The average roof installation in Pennsylvania ranges from $8,000 to $15,000. Depending on the type of shingle and maintenance, your roof can last anywhere from 15 to 40 years.

Need a new roof?

Signs that you might need a new roof:

  • Water stains or leaks inside your home .
  • Missing shingles .
  • Visible wind damage.

If you notice any of these signs, it's time to consider a repair or replacement.
